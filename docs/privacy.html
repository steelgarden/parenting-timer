Privacy Policy

Parenting Timer — Effective 13 March 2026

Overview

Parenting Timer is a simple countdown and stopwatch app. Your privacy is important to us, and this app is designed to respect it fully.

Data Collection

Parenting Timer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No user accounts or registration
  • No network requests (the app works entirely offline)
  • No access to contacts, camera, location, or other device sensors
  • No advertising

Local Storage

The app saves your preferences (such as alert sound choice, timer settings, and speed settings) locally on your device using standard browser/app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

The Android version of the app requests no special permissions beyond basic vibration for timer alerts, which is handled entirely on-device.

Third-Party Services

Parenting Timer uses no third-party services, SDKs, libraries, or APIs. The app is entirely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. It is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the app's GitHub repository.
